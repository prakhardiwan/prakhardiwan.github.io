Prakhar Diwan

Interest Areas - Computer Architecture and Digital Design

I am a digital design engineer (joined: Aug’23) at Texas Instruments, India, working in high-speed data converters group. My work focuses on developing calibration systems for high-speed ADCs: performing device modeling, RTL design, functional verification and synthesis.

I graduated with a Dual Degree (B.Tech+M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology - Bombay, India, with a Minor in Computer Science.

I'm inclined towards research in computer architecture and hardware design. I pursued my master's thesis under guidance of Prof. Virendra Singh , I proposed novel energy-efficient hetereogenous-ISA architecture and scheduling techniques to realize its potential.

I spent my Summer'21 conducting research on ultra-low power neural network accelerators under guidance of Prof. Oliver Bringmann with Embedded Systems group at University of Tübingen, Germany.

I worked as software engineering intern during Summer'20 at Epocare, a deep-tech healthcare startup.
